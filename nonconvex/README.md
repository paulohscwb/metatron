  <h2 align="center"><img src="vr/salas/imagens/icone.png" style="margin-bottom:-10px" width="45"> Metatron's cube and the nonconvex polyhedra 1</h2>
Metatron's Cube is a complex structure of Sacred Geometry, derived from the structure of the Flower of Life. It is named after Archangel Metatron. The Metatron symbol appears in Jewish, Islamic and Christian tradition. This symbol is considered responsible for all creation and is considered an Archangel as well as a judge. 
<br>This work shows nonconvex polyhedra inscribed in the symbol of Metatron's cube, with views that can be accessed with Augmented Reality resources and also in immersive Virtual Reality rooms.
